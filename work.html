How does the Website work?

The website accesses the Accerlerometer, which is integrated in the smartphone. This accelerometer can be used to detect movements. This can have many useful applications, such as caring for a senior citizen in their own home. For example, this sensor can be attached to a wristband which the senior citizens then wear. The wristband can then detect when there has been a rapid acceleration and then no more movement. This indicates a fall of the senior citizen. With the help of such a technology, an early warning system could be developed to ensure that senior citizens do not lie around alone at home for hours after a fall.
